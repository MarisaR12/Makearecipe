♡Chocolate Chip Cookies!♡

Cocolate chip cookies are great cookies for anything. Wanting to celebrate,feeling a little down, just want a cookie, etc. These cookies are amazing for all these type of moods and more so scroll down to figure out how to make them!

Image credits to Sally's baking addiction

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 cup of unsalted melted butter
  • 3/4 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • One large egg
  • one egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/4 cup of milk chocolate chocolate chips

Directions

  1. In a bowl whisk together your flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt together. After whisking leave it along.
  2. In a different bowl whisk together the melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar.
  3. After that whisk in the egg and egg yolk along with the vanilla extract.
  4. Next whisk together in the dry ingredients in with the wet ingrediants until nicely combined.
  5. Then cover the dough and let it sit in the fridge for 2-3 hours.
  6. After 2-3 hours has passed preheat your oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit take the dough out and shape them into medium sized balls.
  7. After oven is done preheating put the cookies in the oven and let it bake for 12-13 minutes.
  8. Take the cookies out after 12-13 minutes and let cool.
  9. After cookies are done cooling serve!
